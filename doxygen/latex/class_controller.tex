\subsection{Detailed Description}
Controllers have the ability to manipulate Squads, Buildings and Units that belong to a \hyperlink{class_team}{Team}, and get them to move around a \hyperlink{class_level}{Level}.

A \hyperlink{class_controller}{Controller} is usually either an \hyperlink{class_a_i_controller}{A\-I\-Controller} (computer-\/controlled) or a \hyperlink{class_player_controller}{Player\-Controller} (human-\/controlled).
